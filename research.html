Smart Data Pricing

Due to the increasing competition in the wireless data market, Mobile Network Operators are under an increasing pressure to increase market shares and improve revenues. A technical approach is to adopt novel wireless technologies to improve the quality of service (QoS) for users. However, the technology upgrade is often costly and time-consuming. A complementary economical approach is to explore various innovative data services to meet the requirements of heterogeneous users. We will design and analyze various smart data pricing mechanisms, such as the time flexible rollover mechanism, the user-flexible family plan and trading platform.
